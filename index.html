Welcome to Super Smash Bros. 4! This is where you can get a great start on this phenomenal game to enjoy it at its peak!

Super Smash Bros. 4 is a 3DS and WiiU game, mainly MMO, that involves the player(s) to choose from a variety of fighters to compete against either AIs or each other. With unique strategic methods from each fighter, there are countless ways to play this game. Not only can you fight your friends with powerful, epic moves, but they can also be utilized in a series of challenges through Classic and All-Star. All of the fighters also come from a variety of popular games, mainly Nintendo, like Marth, Kirby, and Pikachu. Online battles are also available to play for fun and to test your skills. This website will help you get a strong start on this exciting game!

The purpose of this website is to help beginners get a proper and effective start on this game in a more competitive perspective. With the variety of different characters with different styles, each one does require a different battling style, so this website provides basic, simple understanding of that. This website is all you need to get a great sense of SSB4!
