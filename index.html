About Me

As of June 3, 2019, I am building a startup with two friends, and we're based out of SF. Stay tuned. :)

I recently finished my PhD in electrical engineering at Stanford University. While at Stanford, I was a member of David Tse's group, and my research revolved around analysis of single-cell RNA-Seq datasets and understanding deep learning models. During the winter 2016 and spring 2018 quarters, I helped design and teach a new Stanford course on data science for high-throughput sequencing. I have also had the privilege of working for some wonderful organizations during my graduate and undergraduate careers including Grail, Cellular Research, MC10, MIT Lincoln Laboratory, and Dana-Farber. Please see my CV for more details.
